ℹ️ About This Dashboard
What is the Stock Analysis Dashboard?

This is a professional-grade investment analysis tool that combines real-time market data with AI-powered scoring algorithms to identify promising trading opportunities and provide investment recommendations.

Key Features
  • Daily Movers Scanning: Identifies S&P 500 stocks with highest momentum using Finnhub API
  • OHLC Analysis: Fetches 7-day historical price data from yfinance
  • Investment Scoring: AI-powered algorithm scoring based on momentum and volatility
  • Watchlist Tracking: Monitor your favorite established stocks (AAPL, NVDA, MSFT, META, GOOGL)
  • Interactive Visualizations: Professional charts using Chart.js and Plotly
  • Smart Recommendations: Buy, Hold, or Avoid signals based on comprehensive analysis
Data Sources
  • Finnhub API: Real-time stock quotes and market data (https://finnhub.io)
  • yfinance: Historical OHLC data from Yahoo Finance
  • PyTickerSymbols: S&P 500 ticker list management
How It Works
  1. Scan: Analyzes S&P 500 stocks for daily price movements
  2. Fetch: Retrieves 7-day OHLC history for top movers and watchlist stocks
  3. Analyze: Calculates technical indicators: momentum, volatility, moving averages
  4. Score: Assigns investment scores based on momentum and trend stability
  5. Recommend: Generates Buy/Hold/Avoid signals with confidence levels
  6. Visualize: Creates professional charts for data-driven insights
Scoring Algorithm
Movers Score = (Daily % + 7-Day %) × 0.6 + (100 / Volatility) × 0.4
Watchlist Score = (7-Day % × 0.5) + (vs 7-MA % × 0.5)
Buy Signal: Score > 5 (movers) or > 1 (watchlist)
Hold Signal: Score > 2 (movers) or > -1 (watchlist)
Avoid Signal: Score ≤ 2 (movers) or ≤ -1 (watchlist)
Portfolio Strategy

This tool recommends a balanced approach:

  • Movers (5-10% of portfolio): High-risk, high-reward opportunities for active traders
  • Watchlist (40-60% of portfolio): Stable, established holdings for long-term growth
  • Cash (10-20% of portfolio): Reserves for opportunistic investments and risk management
